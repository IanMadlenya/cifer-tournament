Overview

This tournament is designed to teach students about electronic trading platforms and portfolio management.

Each team is given a basket of stocks and options, which must be held for the full duration of the tournament. Teams are also provided $18 million in cash, which they use to hedge their positions. Their initial total portfolio (including cash) is worth $53709280. The team that most closely follows a 1% annualized return over the entire tournament wins.

Rules

  1. For a trade to be initiated, the required amount of cash must be present in the portfolio.
  2. Teams cannot trade assets in their initial portfolio.
  3. Teams may not trade any asset more than once a day.
  4. Teams may not trade any asset that currently has a value of zero dollars.
  5. Teams may not switch from a long position to a short position in the same trade.
  6. The value of short sold assets must not exceed $22 million dollars.
  7. Teams must have at least 30% of all shorted assets' value in cash at all times.
