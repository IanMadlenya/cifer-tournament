Overview

This tournament is designed to teach students about electronic trading platforms and portfolio management.

Each team is given a basket of stocks and options, which must be held for the full duration of the tournament. Teams are also provided cash, which they use to hedge their positions. Their initial total portfolio value will be approximately $50 million. The team that most closely follows a 1% annualized return over the entire tournament wins.

Rules

  1. The value of the margin account must not exceed $22 million dollars.
  2. Teams must have at least 30% of the margin account value in cash at all times.
  3. Teams may not trade any asset more than once a day.
  4. For a trade to be initiated the required amount of cash must be present in the portfolio.
  5. Teams may not trade any asset that has a value of zero dollars.
  6. Teams cannot trade assets in their initial portfolio.
